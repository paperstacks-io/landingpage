paperstacks.io

Find, stack, sort, and share papers for your research.

All scientific knowledge, open to everyone!

The scientific publication ecosystem relies heavily on proprietary platforms for search, indexing, and reference management, resulting in opaque processes, strong vendor lock-in, and limited data sovereignty. At the same time, open alternatives are fragmented, poorly interoperable, and lack reliable linking between different publication versions. Although open metadata sources exist, there is no integrated, transparent, and reproducible infrastructure that supports the full scientific workflow. Consequently, researchers and institutions lack long-term control, interoperability, and clarity over access, versions, and use of scientific publications, especially from a European perspective.

Mission

Our mission is to empower researchers with open, interoperable, and sovereign infrastructure for scientific knowledge.

Technical Demonstrator

paperstacks.io is a user-facing prototype of an open, data-sovereign digital infrastructure for discovering, organizing, validating, and sharing scholarly publications.

Features

Find

  • Discover research articles and references quickly.
  • Keep all papers relevant to your research in one place.
  • Search across titles, authors, and metadata.

Share

  • Share paper stacks with collaborators.
  • Create public links for open access papers.
  • Simple permissions for controlled collaboration.

Stack & Sort

  • Organize papers into clear, structured paper stacks.
  • Automatic metadata cleanup for accurate, consistent references.
  • Duplicate detection to keep your library clean and reliable.

Notes & Highlights

  • Highlight PDFs and attach notes directly to papers.
  • Keep quotes, snippets, and thoughts next to the source.
  • Everything stays searchable and connected to the reference.

Access Everywhere

  • Cloud-based access on web, desktop, and mobile.
  • No setup or configuration required.
  • Your library is always up to date across devices.

Add & Import

  • Add PDFs or BibTeX files in seconds.
  • Migrate your entire library from Zotero, EndNote, or Mendeley.
  • Simple, frictionless onboarding for existing workflows.

Privacy-First

  • No ads, trackers, or behavioral analytics.
  • Your research data stays private and under your control.
  • Export your data anytime.

Open Source

  • Fully open-source and transparent.
  • Built with and for the research community.
  • No lock-in, your library is future-proof.
